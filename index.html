Philosophy

Your body already
knows how to feel

Most of us were taught to live from the neck up. To manage our bodies rather than inhabit them. We learned to perform desire instead of listening to it, to override sensation with expectation, to treat pleasure as something earned rather than something inherent.

This is bodywork at the intersection of somatic therapy, tantric tradition and erotic intelligence. It is not about fixing you. It is about returning you to what was never actually lost: the alive, sensing, desiring animal that you are.

About

Working with
James

I came to this work through my own body. Through years of unlearning the numbness I had been carrying, and through the slow, sometimes messy process of reclaiming my own erotic wholeness as a bisexual man.

That journey taught me something I bring to every session: that healing happens when we stop apologising for what we feel. When we let pleasure be intelligent, and let desire be a teacher.

I hold space for people of all genders and orientations. My practice is rooted in tantric bodywork, somatic therapy and a naturist ethos that treats the naked body as simply the body. Neither spectacle nor shame. I am warm, direct, boundaried and present.
